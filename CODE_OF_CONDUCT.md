# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the Echo Universe ecosystem a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Positive: welcoming language, respecting viewpoints, accepting criticism, showing empathy.
Unacceptable: trolling, harassment, publishing private info without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers will take corrective action for unacceptable behavior.

**Contact**: echonate@echouniverse.org

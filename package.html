What to do with these documents
  1. File Intent to File NOW at VA.gov if you haven't — locks in your effective date for back pay
  2. Submit SF-180 and NPRC requests — mail or fax to get your military records
  3. Submit VA Form 21-4142 — release forms so VA can pull your private medical records
  4. Take nexus letter template to your doctor — ask them to complete and sign it on their letterhead
  5. Have buddies sign their statements — notarized if possible
  6. File VA Form 21-526EZ — attach everything once you have it all

This package is a preparation tool, not legal advice. A VSO (DAV, American Legion, VFW) will file for free and can review your complete package before submission.

## What This Is

Echo VA Claims is a standalone application for veterans preparing VA disability claims.

It is **Domain 1** of the Art of Proof evidence preparation ecosystem — extracted into its own
product with its own brand, its own URL, and its own identity.

**This app is for veterans.**
Art of Proof is for everyone.
They share an engine. They serve different people.

---

## What It Does

**Evidence scoring:** 21 document types, individual weights, completeness/quality/consistency scoring.

**Condition mapping:** Every primary condition maps to secondaries that can also be claimed.
Most veterans leave money on the table because they do not know the secondary map.

**Nexus generation:** AI-generated nexus letters per condition — the medical-legal argument
connecting your current condition to your service.

**The 13-Point Devil Lens:** Proprietary validation before any package is generated:
source purity · regulatory basis · constructive possession · chronological continuity ·
jurisdictional lock · effective-date anchor · anti-development shield · accountability clause ·
hazard/ADL mapping · constructive narrative · denial countermeasure · record-lock statement ·
retroactive impact.

**Package generation:** Complete 13-document VA submission packet with CFR citations,
legal brief, nexus letters, and pre-formatted request letters for missing documents.

**Attorney referral trigger:** When your score reaches 40%+, the system shows you when
professional help would change your outcome — and connects you to accredited representation.

## The Document That Changes Everything

Most veterans do not know this document exists.

The **MEPS entry physical** (DD Form 2808) is the medical examination taken when you
enlisted. It documents your health baseline before service. If any condition worsened
during service, this document is the anchor that proves the before-and-after.

Echo VA Claims shows you this document exists, shows you how to request it, and generates
the pre-formatted letter to the National Personnel Records Center.

This is what "we show you what rock to look under" means.

Echo VA Claims is Domain 1 of the Art of Proof evidence ecosystem.
